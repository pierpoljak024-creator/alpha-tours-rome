## FAQ's

## Frequently Asked Questions

We understand that planning a trip to Rome — or anywhere in Italy — often comes with many questions. At **Rome Tour Guide**, we want to make your journey as smooth and stress-free as possible.

[Contact Us](https://rome-tourguide.com/contact-us/)

[How many people can fit in one golf cart?](https://rome-tourguide.com/#collapse-4c2774c69c01719a9300)

Each golf cart can accommodate up to 7 people including infants.

[Can I customize my tour?](https://rome-tourguide.com/#collapse-a60267d69c01719a9300)

Of course! We specialize in private and personalized itineraries to fit your preferences.

[Do you provide transportation?](https://rome-tourguide.com/#collapse-4a5f00869c01719a9300)

Yes, we offer luxury vans and chauffeur services for a comfortable travel experience.

[Are your guides licensed professionals?](https://rome-tourguide.com/#collapse-d28823769c01719a9300)

Yes, all our guides are fully licensed and certified, with deep knowledge of history, culture, and local traditions.

[What languages do your guides speak?](https://rome-tourguide.com/#collapse-008c0d969c01719a9300)

Our guides speak English and Italian.

[Do you offer skip-the-line access?](https://rome-tourguide.com/#collapse-9d5feb569c01719a9300)

Absolutely! Our VIP tours include priority access to avoid long queues.

[What happens in case of bad weather?](https://rome-tourguide.com/#collapse-f68464369c01719a9300)

Tours operate rain or shine, but if conditions are unsafe, we offer rescheduling or a refund

[What is your cancellation policy?](https://rome-tourguide.com/#collapse-3a7803969c01719a9300)

You can cancel up to 24 hours before the tour for a full refund. Cancellations made less than 24 hours in advance are non-refundable.
