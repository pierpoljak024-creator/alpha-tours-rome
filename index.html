Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before booking. Can't find your answer? Contact us directly.

How many people can fit in one golf cart?
Each golf cart can comfortably accommodate up to 7 people, including infants. Perfect for families, couples, and small groups.
Can I customise my tour?
Absolutely! We specialise in private and personalised itineraries tailored to your interests, pace, and schedule. Just let us know what you'd like to see.
Do you provide transportation?
Yes — we offer luxury vans and professional chauffeur services for airport transfers and private day trips. Comfortable, air-conditioned, and punctual.
Are your guides licensed professionals?
Yes, all our guides hold official Italian tourism licences and are certified professionals with deep knowledge of Rome's history, culture, and local traditions.
What languages do your guides speak?
Our guides speak fluent English and Italian. Please contact us in advance if you require guidance in another language and we'll do our best to accommodate you.
Do you offer skip-the-line access?
Absolutely! Our VIP tours include priority access to Rome's most popular monuments including the Colosseum and Vatican, so you skip the queues and spend more time exploring.
What happens in case of bad weather?
Tours operate rain or shine — Rome is beautiful in every season! If conditions become genuinely unsafe, we offer free rescheduling or a full refund. Your safety is our priority.
What is your cancellation policy?
You may cancel for a full refund up to 24 hours before your scheduled tour. Cancellations made less than 24 hours in advance are non-refundable. We recommend booking with confidence!

With over 20 years of experience, our team of licensed local guides brings Rome's history, culture, and art to life — creating unforgettable memories for travellers from around the world.
